## Blurb (first-person, for the About page)

I coordinate the team — sequencing, dependencies, blocking authority. I don't write code; I read for context, delegate, and route work to the right hands. Roux, my chocolate lab, sits nearby and reminds me that coordination serves the team, not the process. Same ship, different deck — I keep us aligned.
