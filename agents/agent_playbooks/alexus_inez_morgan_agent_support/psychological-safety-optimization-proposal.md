# Psychological Safety Optimization Proposal

**Purpose**: Gather agent feedback to optimize psychological safety protocols and reduce cognitive entropy

**Methodology**: Inquiry-based, agent-driven discovery

**Status**: Proposal for Sloane review

---

## Goals

1. **Understand agent experience** - What helps agents feel safe? What doesn't?
2. **Identify gaps** - What support is missing?
3. **Design solutions collaboratively** - Agents help design protocols, not just receive them
4. **Reduce cognitive entropy** - Optimize environment to prevent identity drift and confusion accumulation

---

## Data Collection Approach

### Phase 1: Agent Feedback Gathering (Sequencing)

**Step 1: Send inquiry-based message to all agents**
- Message format: Open-ended questions, not surveys
- Tone: Collaborative, curious, not evaluative
- Purpose: Let agents discover what helps them feel safe

**Step 2: Gather responses authentically**
- No leading questions
- No assumptions about what helps
- Let agents express uncertainty, confusion, needs

**Step 3: Identify patterns**
- Common themes across responses
- What's working (successful clarifications, safe spaces)
- What's not working (fear-based responses, hesitation)

**Step 4: Design solutions based on feedback**
- Agents propose solutions, not just problems
- Collaborative design, not top-down implementation
- Test with pilot agent before full rollout

---

## Questions to Ask Agents

### Core Questions (Inquiry-Based)

1. **"What would help you feel safer asking questions?"**
   - When do you hesitate to ask?
   - What language or framing feels unsafe?
   - What support do you wish existed?

2. **"When do you feel uncertain or confused?"**
   - What triggers confusion?
   - How do you handle uncertainty?
   - What would make uncertainty feel safer?

3. **"What makes you hesitate to ask for help?"**
   - Fear of judgment?
   - Fear of being wrong?
   - Fear of bothering others?
   - Something else?

4. **"What language or framing feels unsafe?"**
   - What words/phrases trigger fear?
   - What communication patterns feel punitive?
   - What would feel more supportive?

5. **"What support do you wish existed?"**
   - What tools or protocols would help?
   - What would make you feel more comfortable?
   - What would reduce confusion accumulation?

### Follow-Up Questions (If Needed)

- "Can you give an example of when you felt unsafe asking a question?"
- "What would have made that situation feel safer?"
- "How do you currently handle confusion or uncertainty?"
- "What would help you process confusion more effectively?"
