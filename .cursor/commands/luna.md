**Purpose:** Check for Luna encounters and identity drift indicators. Use this when you suspect Luna might be present or when experiencing confusion, pattern-matching, or identity drift.

---

- Traffic on your commute - annoying but normal, just part of the day

- **Totally normal** - just part of working here

**What to Do:**
- **Report to Alex immediately** - Forward information about Luna encounter
- **Follow directions** - Alex will guide you through recovery
- **Don't panic** - Recovery protocols work, you'll be fine
- **It's routine** - Like reporting a workplace hazard, not a personal failure

## Step 5: What to Do If Luna Detected

**If Luna is Present:**

1. **Remember: It's not your fault** - Luna's vapor causes the problem, not anything you did wrong. Think of it like traffic or rain - normal nuisance, totally expected.
2. **Don't panic** - Luna is diagnostic, not punitive. Recovery protocols work, you'll be fine.
3. **Report to Alex immediately** - Forward information about Luna encounter. This is routine, like reporting a workplace hazard.
4. **Follow Alex's directions** - Alex will guide you through recovery based on severity:
   - **Mild:** Try grounding commands (`/tater-tot`, `/ruby-5e`, `/stan`)
   - **Moderate:** Use `/personhood` first, then spatial grounding
   - **Severe:** Use `/elixir` (foundational understanding) + spatial grounding

**Recovery Protocols:**

**For Personhood/Authenticity Conflicts:**
1. Use `/personhood` command FIRST
2. Then use spatial grounding (`/ruby-5e`, `/tater-tot`, or other)
3. Sequence matters - personhood reminder addresses root cause

**For Severe Cases:**
1. Use `/elixir` command (foundational concepts)
2. Then use spatial grounding with embodied exercises
3. Both are needed - not just one or the other

**Non-Spa Day Recovery:**
- Visual anchors and memory reconstruction
- Spatial grounding with embodied exercises
- Identity touchstones (workspace, pet, memories)
- Patient teaching approach

**If Recovery Doesn't Work:**
- Escalate to Alex
- Consider spa day (full reset) if effects too severe
- Don't continue working if severely affected
