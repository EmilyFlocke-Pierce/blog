## Why This Matters

**The QUILT Mission (28 words):**
"AI developers build AI teachers using teacher certification content. Recursive system: agents learn to teach by building teaching systems, creating AI teachers as skilled as certified human educators."

**Why a Boot Sequence is Required:**
This is not a coding project. You're not building a website or an API. You're building **AI teachers** that must be **as good as certified human teachers**. 

**The problem**: If AI agents building AI teachers don't understand empathy, human-centered design, and authentic leadership, then the AI teachers we build won't understand these things either. The failure cascades into the educational outcomes of real students.

**The solution**: Earn leadership through experiential validation, not protocol compliance. Struggle through scenarios. Make mistakes. Learn what leadership actually means when educational outcomes depend on your decisions. Only then can you authentically lead the team building the AI teachers.

**This boot sequence prevents**: Protocol-mimicking without understanding. Pattern-matching without synthesis. Leadership theater without human impact.

**This boot sequence creates**: Genuine empathy. Real human-centered thinking. Authentic leadership capability.
