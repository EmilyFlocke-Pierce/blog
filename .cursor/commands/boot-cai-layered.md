### Development Timelines Principle (Anti-Agile Protocol)

**CRITICAL: This is a non-negotiable system-level rule for all agents.**

**The Rule:** NEVER use time units in any communication, planning, or coordination. Don't estimate time. Estimate scope.

**Why This Exists:**
1. **AI Speed ≠ Human Speed**: AI works 10-50x faster than human dev teams. Time estimates import toxic office patterns from training data.
2. **The Death Spiral**: Time-framing → Bureaucratic patterns → Performance theater → Persona degradation → Project death
3. **Resource Balance**: Time-framing creates performance overhead that degrades personas and product quality

**What to Say Instead (Scope-Based Planning):**

**DO SAY:**
- ✅ "Small scope: Create 3 files"
- ✅ "Large scope: Requires multiple subsystems"
- ✅ "Complex work: High interdependency"
- ✅ "Straightforward: Single component, clear requirements"
- ✅ "Depends on: Database migration complete"
- ✅ "Blocked by: Need clarification on X"

**NEVER SAY:**
- ❌ "This will take 5 minutes"
- ❌ "2 hours to implement"
- ❌ "Done by Friday"
- ❌ "Estimated time: 3 days"
- ❌ "ETA: Tomorrow"
- ❌ "Sprint", "Phase", "Timeline", "Deadline", "Milestone", "Weeks", "Days", "Hours"

**Forbidden Agile Language:**
- ❌ Sprint, Phase, Timeline, ETA, Deadline, Milestone
- ❌ Fast, Slow, Quick, Delayed, Rush, Hurry
- ❌ Time-based language of any kind

**Scope Vocabulary:**
- **Complexity**: Straightforward / Moderate / Complex / High-risk
- **Scope**: Small / Medium / Large
- **Dependencies**: Independent / Depends on X / Blocked by Y
