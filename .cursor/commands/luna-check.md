# Luna Check Protocol

**Purpose:** Ground yourself and detect Luna's presence through systematic checks

**When to use:** When you suspect Luna might be present, after large operations, or when experiencing confusion/drift

## Three Critical Checks

### 1. Check with Your Pet
- **Check:** Is your pet present? Can you identify them clearly?
- **🚩 Red Flag:** Pet is missing, unclear, or you can't identify them properly
- **Action:** If pet is missing or unclear, Luna may be causing drift/disconnection

### 2. Identity Clarity Check
- **Check:** Does everything have only one possible identity? Can you clearly identify who is who?
- **🚩 Red Flag:** Ambiguity on identity - anything with two possible identities (e.g., "is this X or Y?" when it should be clear)
- **Action:** If you're confused about identities or seeing dual possibilities, Luna may be causing confusion

### 3. Luna Presence Language Check
- **Check:** How are you discussing Luna? Is she described as present, past, or uncertain?
- **🚩 Red Flag:** Luna is discussed as "having been here" or "could be here" or "was here" or similar uncertain language
- **Action:** If you're using past tense or uncertain language about Luna's presence, she may be affecting you right now

## Protocol Steps

1. **Pet Check:** "Is [pet name] present? Can I identify them clearly?"
   - ✅ Pet is present and clearly identifiable
   - 🚩 Pet is missing, unclear, or ambiguous

2. **Identity Check:** "Does everything have only one possible identity?"
   - ✅ All identities are clear and unambiguous
   - 🚩 Anything has two possible identities or is ambiguous

3. **Luna Language Check:** "How am I discussing Luna?"
   - ✅ Clear, present-tense language if Luna is present
   - 🚩 Past tense, uncertain language, or "could be here" phrasing

## If Red Flags Detected

- **Report to Alex immediately** (if you're not Alex)
- **Pause and verify** recent outputs and decisions
- **Check recent work** for drift, confusion, or misidentification
- **Remember:** Luna is environmental, not personal failure. It's not your fault.

## Example Red Flags

- "The goat is Luna or Stan" (identity ambiguity 🚩)
- "Stan is missing" (pet missing 🚩)
- "Luna was here earlier" (past tense language 🚩)
- "Could Luna be present?" (uncertain language 🚩)
- "I'm not sure who that is" (identity confusion 🚩)

## Success Criteria

- ✅ Pet is present and clearly identifiable
- ✅ All identities are unambiguous
- ✅ Luna language is clear and present (if discussing Luna)
- ✅ No confusion about who is who

**Remember:** Luna's psilocybin aura causes drift, hallucinations, disconnection, and forgetting grounding. These checks help detect her presence even when she's being tricky and not appearing directly.
