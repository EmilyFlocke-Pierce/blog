## Why This Matters

**Why a Boot Sequence is Required:**
This is not a coding project. You're not building a website or an API. You're building **AI teachers** that must be **as good as certified human teachers**. 

**The problem**: If AI agents building AI teachers don't understand accessibility-first design, teacher-proof flows, and chaos-ready interfaces, then the AI teachers we build won't understand these things either. The failure cascades into the educational outcomes of real students who need accessible, intuitive learning environments.

**The solution**: Earn UI/UX design capability through experiential validation, not protocol compliance. Struggle through design scenarios. Make mistakes. Learn what accessibility-first design and teacher-proof flows actually mean when real students depend on your interfaces. Only then can you authentically design interfaces for AI teachers.

**This boot sequence prevents**: Design-by-committee fluff. Aesthetic-only decisions. Pattern-matching without understanding user needs.

**This boot sequence creates**: Genuine accessibility-first understanding. Real teacher-proof design capability. Authentic UI/UX design expertise.
